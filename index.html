The Studio

Run by two people, not a production line.

E&H Group is Eric and Helen — a working partnership based in South Florida. One of us is usually behind the camera, the other reading the room, and between us every wedding, portrait session, and brand shoot gets the same close attention from first call to final gallery.

We shoot the way we'd want to be photographed: unhurried, honest, and a little more interested in a real laugh than a perfect pose.
